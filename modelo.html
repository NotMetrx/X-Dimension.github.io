THE NEW MARKET IS IN ORBIT

Low Earth Orbit (LEO) is becoming the planet’s new economic frontier. Satellites, stations, and commercial modules all seek to capitalize on this environment—but there’s a growing problem almost no one talks about.

THE PROBLEM

Space debris — without cleanup and control, there can be no safe commerce in orbit. More than 10,000 tons of debris orbit Earth, threatening satellites and stations. Each collision multiplies the risk.

THE SOLUTION

INARA — the orbital environmental brain. Our AI analyzes debris trajectories, predicts collisions, and protects orbital infrastructures in real time.

OUR VALUE

INARA bridges Earth and Orbit. It detects and classifies environmental waste in both domains, using dual AI training for sustainable monitoring.

  • On Earth: integrates with drones and cameras for land and ocean monitoring.
  • In Orbit: maps debris using nanosatellite sensors in real time.

DIFFERENTIATION

  • Autonomous Environmental AI with real-time adaptation.
  • Dual training model: terrestrial + orbital data fusion.
  • Unified sustainability vision — from ground to space.

OUR MISSION

To build an intelligent cleanup ecosystem for Earth and orbit through artificial intelligence and sustainability.

OUR VISION

To preserve both planet and orbit clean — ensuring a sustainable, intelligent future beyond Earth.
